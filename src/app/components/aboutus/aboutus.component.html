About Us

Sundus Elektronik Para ve Ödeme Kuruluşu A.Ş. is a payment institution, licensed by the Central Bank of Turkey, established for the purpose of carrying out money transfer activities as specified in Paragraph 1 (Ç) of Article 12 of the Law No. 6493 on Payment and Securities Settlement Systems, Payment Services and Electronic Money Institutions.

You can quickly transfer money at our branches. We welcome you to SundusPay to make your money transfers with pleasure and confidence for a completely different application experience.
